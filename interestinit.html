My interest in

Information Technology

Artificial Intelligence and Game Development are the two fields of Information Technology (I.T.) that I am most interested in. My interest in Game Development started when I was a young boy playing classic videogames from the 90s and 2000s: Doom, Donkey Kong, Super Mario, Pacman, Touhou, Counter Strike, Civilization and Age of Empires… More recently during the 2010s, my interests in game shifted to independent platformer/side-scroller titles like Ori and the Blind Forest, Hue, Hollow Knight and Celeste… The rise of smartphones and digital personal assistants such as Siri and Cortana also piqued my insterest in machine learning and artificial intelligence, particularly in its applications to serve human needs, modern automation and so much more…

I started to seriously study programming since I was 12, and had familiarized myself with C, Python and Java. I had a break during the 2017 – 2019 period to pursue my interests in music and design, but ultimately, I decided to come back into I.T. following the current employment trend. Though I am no longer have the burning passion I once had for I.T., I still love to contribute my skills and ideas to the community and use I.T. as a tool to connect people regardless of race, gender or wealth so that we can build a better future together.

Before coming to RMIT University Vietnam, I spent some time abroad in the United States and Singapore. It was when I came back home that I realized the immense potential of the mainland Vietnamese developing economy and culture. Studying at RMIT is a chance for me to develop my skills working in a multi-cultural, diverse environment with unfiltered ideas from around the globe. With that, I want to dedicate what I learnt to bring the world to Vietnam and bring Vietnamese culture to the world.

In the upcoming years of my study at RMIT Vietnam, with the help from our staff and faculty, I aim to utilize the most of my resources to make myself a better software developer, a better leader and a better learner overall.
